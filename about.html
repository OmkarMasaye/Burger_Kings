About Jumbo King

Our Story, Mission & Values

Our Story

Founded in 2001, Jumbo King began with a simple vision: to bring affordable, delicious, and high-quality burgers to every Indian household. What started as a small outlet in Mumbai has now grown into a beloved fast-food chain with 50+ outlets across India.

Our journey has been driven by passion, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to serve the best burgers that combine Indian flavors with global quality standards.

Our Mission

To serve happiness on a bun! We believe in creating moments of joy through our delicious burgers, made fresh with the finest ingredients. Our mission is to provide:

  • 🍔 Fresh, high-quality ingredients in every burger
  • 💰 Affordable pricing for everyone
  • 😊 Exceptional customer service
  • 🌟 Unique Indian-inspired flavors
  • 🏪 Accessible outlets across major cities
